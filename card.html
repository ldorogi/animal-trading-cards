The Mini Lop is a very popular rabbit breed. The Mini Lop rabbit is classified as a medium-sized rabbit. Senior Bucks and Does must be six months of age and older and weigh no more than 6.5 pounds (3 kg). Ideal weight is 5.5 pounds (2.5 kg). Junior Bucks and Does must be under six months of age and weigh less than 6 pounds (2.7 kg). Like any animal, Mini Lops come in a variety of personalities but they are generally fairly friendly and extremely playful. Like all rabbits, they may be taught a variety of tricks and commands. They can be trained to respond to a clicker.
